1-Introduction¶

This dataset contains data of around 110527 medical appointments and whether the patient showed up or not.¶

1-PatientId : A unique number for each patient¶
2-AppointmentID : Re-attendance of the patient for examination¶
3-Gender : Male and Female¶
4-ScheduledDay :booking date¶
5-AppointmentDay : Attendance date¶
6-Age : patient's age¶
7-Neighbourhood :Patient's place of residence¶
8-Scholarship :This patient took his Scholarship or not¶
9-Hipertension :Do you have high blood pressure or not?¶
10-Diabetes :diabetic or not¶
11- Alcoholism :Alcoholic or not?¶
12-Handcap :Does he have a Handcap or not?¶
13-SMS_received :Message received or not¶
14-No-show :attended or not¶
